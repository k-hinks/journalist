I'm a Montreal-based journalist who has worked at the Montreal Gazette and Toronto Star. I currently work on the web desk at CTV Montreal and I'm the associate editor at Montreal Families. I also freelance for various publications.

I grew up just outside of Montreal. Swearing in franglais is one of my true passions.

My favourite things to write and read about involve mental health issues, bilingualism issues and sports, especially baseball.
